Touba Mosq in 1919

The Great Mosque of Touba is a mosque in Touba, Senegal. It was founded by Amadou Bamba in 1887 and completed in 1963. Bamba died in 1927 and is interred inside the mosque. Since his death the mosque is being controlled by his family.Today Touba represents a huge patrimoin that inspires many people in Senegal, mostly in the Muslim community
